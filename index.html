RPM that Actually Works

Modern Nursing demands more than care. It demands systems that think ahead.

Dozee is an automated remote patient monitoring platform that uses under-the-mattress bed sensors to capture vital signs through continuous monitoring. Its advanced early warning system helps clinicians detect patient deterioration early, without physical contact or manual checks.
